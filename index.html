This is Us!

Kourtney Vaughn

CEO and athletic trainer

Igor Porciuncula is a physical therapist from Brazil, swim instructor, and Red Cross lifeguard. I have enjoyed being in the water since I took my first swim lesson at around 6 years old. Since then, swimming has been a big part of my life. Swimming has always brought me a lot of joy, from my early days on a youth swim team to my career as a swim instructor today. Working at the Swim Academy of current 4-time gold medalist Lenny Krayzelburg for 9 years has giving me extensive knowledge of swimming and taught me a lot about the sport. Also, my background as a physical therapist gives me a unique perspective on developing this exercise program and meeting each client's physical needs.

Aquarobics

Tone and sculpt your body with no impact to your joints. This popular water aerobics workout is a swimming blend of cardio and resistance training and may incorporate resistance tools such as buoyant water weights and noodles. Great for all ages and physical strength. Safe for expecting mothers senior citizens and those recovering from an injury

Aqua-Bootcamp

NOT YOUR GRANDMAS WATER AEROBICS CLASS!! HIGH INTENSITY water fitness program that provides the benefits of a pool, with little to no impact on joints, as well as the benefits of weight and resistance training of a traditional gym.This program is UNIQUE because it is a rigorous cross-training of swimming, water polo, water aerobics, modified plyometrics, and traditional strength and cardiovascular exercises. Unlike many water based fitness programs that target mainly senior citizens and people recovering from injury, Aqua bootcamp sets new standards in water-based fitness that CHALLENGES swimmers of all ages and physical shape. Whether you are a recreational swimmer or you have an extensive aquatic background, the hour-long session will PUSH YOU TO YOUR LIMIT.

Aqua-Zumba

brings new meaning to the idea of an invigorating workout. It combines the South American Zumba rhythm and dance steps with a pool party. It offers a fun but challenging, water-based, body-toning workout. Follow along to the latest hits from Latin to hip hop music and enjoy the benefits of increased calorie burn like never before from the waters resistance.
