Our Team

This is Us!

Kourtney Vaughn

ALIVE ELITE CEO and athletic trainer

Born in and raised in Miami Florida, I grew up having a passion for fitness. I started athletic training at the age of 16 pursuing a career in track and field. My career in this sport lasted 7 years putting district champion, 100 meter record holder, and 2 time state qualifier under my belt. After competing for several years, I focused my energy on creating a training program that would not only change the physical health of athletes and the average person but also the mental health. With my experience I realized that health and wellness could only be achieved through both physical and mental strength. Our program is designed to fit the needs of every person, from struggling with weight loss, balancing nutrition, recovering from injury to athletic fitness and mental health we identify your wellness goals and guide you to achieving your best life.

Alexandra Camacho

ALIVE Aquatic trainer

I have enjoyed being in the water Since the age of 3. From being on the youth swim team and competing all the way through college I have acquired extensive amounts of knowledge about the sport. At the age of 16 I perused a career as a certified swim instructor and red cross lifeguard. I fell in love with the impact I had on the lives that I taught and saved. I like to say that this wear my relationship with the water changed, it went from being just a sport to life or death situations. I realized that teaching water skills could save the lives of many and give mobility back to those who want and need it most. I then dedicated my career to creating a program that offered aquatic training to every age and level of swimmer. From infant resource training, to stroke clinics and water fitness/rehabilitation Alive Elite offers it all.

Jade Ingraham

ALIVE Counselor

My passion for empowering others through counseling and coaching began as a graduate of Coral Reef Senior High, where I majored in the Medicine and Health Science academy. It is here I realized that mental health played a huge role in the wellness of every person. I became a certified Life Coach through the Life Coach Training Institute a few years later and have dedicated my life to helping others achieve the life they deserve. I take pride in my spiritual upbringing and use this to guide me in assisting others. Respectfully so I embrace differences and remain open-minded to secular ideas, reasonings, perspectives, and moral values. I have created a program that honors the differences in culture, esteem matters of psychological disorders, and relationships.

Aquarobics

Tone and sculpt your body with no impact to your joints. This popular water aerobics workout is a swimming blend of cardio and resistance training and may incorporate resistance tools such as buoyant water weights and noodles. Great for all ages and physical strength. Safe for expecting mothers senior citizens and those recovering from an injury

Aqua-Bootcamp

NOT YOUR GRANDMAS WATER AEROBICS CLASS!! HIGH INTENSITY water fitness program that provides the benefits of a pool, with little to no impact on joints, as well as the benefits of weight and resistance training of a traditional gym.This program is UNIQUE because it is a rigorous cross-training of swimming, water polo, water aerobics, modified plyometrics, and traditional strength and cardiovascular exercises. Unlike many water based fitness programs that target mainly senior citizens and people recovering from injury, Aqua bootcamp sets new standards in water-based fitness that CHALLENGES swimmers of all ages and physical shape. Whether you are a recreational swimmer or you have an extensive aquatic background, the hour-long session will PUSH YOU TO YOUR LIMIT.

Aqua-Zumba

brings new meaning to the idea of an invigorating workout. It combines the South American Zumba rhythm and dance steps with a pool party. It offers a fun but challenging, water-based, body-toning workout. Follow along to the latest hits from Latin to hip hop music and enjoy the benefits of increased calorie burn like never before from the waters resistance.

Corporate + Residential

Train Harder Live easier!!

Alive Elites Corporate and residential programs was Created based on the theory that all people have the potential to perform well in all aspects of their lives. These people just needed a way in which they could control their own health needs and be in a suitable position to do so. Alive Elites goal is to bring health and wellness to the fore front of our communities by providing something for every level and need of an individual. By doing this, people from all walks of life can improve their health no matter if they’re a computer programmer or basketball player, a mom to be, recovering from injury, in the best shape of their life, suffering from depression, battling obesity the list goes on. Each individual just needs the right fitness challenge, ideas, and plans to give them the boost they need to be more physically and mentally healthy.
